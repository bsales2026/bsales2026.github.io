An Introduction To Chess

Chess is a game played between two opponents on opposite sides of a board containing 64 squares of alternating colors. Each player has 16 pieces: 1 king, 1 queen, 2 rooks, 2 bishops, 2 knights, and 8 pawns. How Does a Chess Game End? the King of one side is captured(Checkmate), or the contestants agree to a draw issue.
